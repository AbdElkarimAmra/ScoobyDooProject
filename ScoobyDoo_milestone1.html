For this project, I will focus on 4 variables:

imdb: the IMDB rating of the episode. This is my response variable.

batman, hex_girls, scrappy_doo: guest characters. These are my main predictor variables.

Both Batman and Hex Girls episodes appear to have higher median rating than episodes without these characters. So it is clear that having these characters show up in the episodes boosted the ratings overall. On the other hand, for ScrappyDoo, the median rating is slighlty lower than episodes without him. This might indicate that this character is not as popular among ScoobyDoo viewers.
